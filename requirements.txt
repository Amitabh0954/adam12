### Summary

This implementation of modifying the quantity of products in the shopping cart ensures that:
- The quantity must be a positive integer.
- Modifying the quantity recalculates the total price automatically.
- API endpoints are provided to handle the modification of product quantities in the shopping cart.
- The MySQL database schema supports necessary fields for cart management.

With these elements in place, the shopping cart functionality will allow users to adjust their order quantities efficiently and manage their purchases flexibly.
