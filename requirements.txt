### Summary

This implementation of adding new products ensures that:
- Product names are unique.
- Product prices are positive numbers.
- Product descriptions are non-empty.
- API endpoint is provided to handle product management.
- The MySQL database schema is updated to accommodate product information.

With these elements in place, the product management functionality will allow administrators to add new products to the inventory, providing essential catalog management capabilities.
