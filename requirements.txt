### Summary

This implementation of removing products from the shopping cart ensures that:
- Users can remove products with a confirmation.
- The total price updates automatically after the item is removed.
- API endpoints are provided to handle the removal of products from the shopping cart.
- The MySQL database schema supports necessary fields for cart management.

With these elements in place, the shopping cart functionality will allow users to manage their selections efficiently and enhance their shopping experience.
