### Summary

This implementation ensures that:
- Products can be updated with valid data by an admin.

- Product descriptions can be modified but not removed.
- Necessary API endpoints are provided for updating products.
- The MySQL database schema supports necessary fields for managing products.

This will enable admins to maintain accurate and current product information, ensuring high-quality management of the product catalog.
