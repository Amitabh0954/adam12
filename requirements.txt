### Summary

- **Shopping Cart Models**: Define shopping carts and cart items.
- **Schemas**: Validate shopping cart data.
- **Services**: Handle cart operations (adding items, viewing cart).
- **Controllers**: Expose endpoints for interacting with shopping carts.
- **Routes**: Include shopping cart routes in the app.
- **Database Initialization**: Ensure models are created and schema is applied.
- **Schema Definitions**: SQL scripts for table creation.

This implementation will ensure that the shopping cart functionality is integrated well into the existing structure, persistent for logged-in users, and provides temporary shopping cart functionality for guest users.
