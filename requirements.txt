### Summary

This implementation of saving and retrieving the shopping cart state for logged-in users ensures that:
- The cart state is saved to the user's profile.
- The saved cart is retrievable across multiple sessions.
- API endpoints are provided to handle saving and retrieving the cart state.
- The MySQL database schema supports necessary fields for cart management.

With these elements in place, users will have their shopping cart state saved and retrievable, enhancing their convenience and loyalty.
